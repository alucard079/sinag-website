Kickoff Year · 2026

Each year, SINAG illuminates a new path forward with a unique theme. For 2026 — its kickoff year — we introduce Sumiklab, Mag-Alab, Sumilab! inspired by SINAG's Core Values.

This theme encourages the youth to recognize that they are the hope of a brighter tomorrow. It reinforces the idea that every person has the power to create a lasting impact, not only for themselves, but also for those who come after them.

By choosing to stand up, grow, and lead, the youth become the torchbearers of transformation. This year's concert serves as an inspiring reminder that we are called to light the path as we walk on in our purpose.

Why This Theme Matters

Many teenagers engage in risky behaviors — including unplanned relationships and early pregnancies — not out of recklessness, but in a search for validation, belonging, and self-worth. When young people lack a sense of purpose or feel pressured by society, they may make decisions with long-term consequences.

The SINAG 2026 concert was conceived as a movement to affirm the value of every young person — to encourage self-respect, inspire informed choices, and emphasize that the decisions they make today shape their future. The theme reinforces the message that their future is in their hands and that they are empowered to empower others.

"Knowing their worth is the first step toward shaping their future wisely."
