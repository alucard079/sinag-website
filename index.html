About SINAG

Hope begins with one ray of light

SINAG stands for a ray of light—warmth, guidance, and momentum. The concert is more than a show; it is a movement that encourages, engages, and empowers the youth through life groups, storytelling, and advocacy. Piliin ang Bukas reminds every teen that knowing their worth and choosing wisely today protects their future.

Encourage

Life & support groups, consolidation, and mentorship keep teens grounded in safe communities.

Engage

Workshops, collaborations, and creative sessions turn talents into meaningful service.

Empower

Advocacy campaigns, volunteer drives, and leadership labs sustain the blaze beyond the concert day.

  • Teenage pregnancy awareness framed with empathy.

  • Ripple effect—each participant becomes light for their circles.
