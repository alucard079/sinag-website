Core Values

Three sparks. One movement.

01
✦
Siklab

Pag-asa ng Kabataan — The Youth's Ignition of Hope

Hope begins with a spark. Every young person deserves to be seen, heard, and supported. SINAG fosters a culture of inclusivity, encouragement, and belonging where no one is left behind.

  • Life & Support Groups
  • Consolidation & Mentorship
  • Outreach Programs
02
◈
Alab

Epekto ng Kabataan — The Youth's Burning Impact

From discovery to action, the youth are empowered to make an impact. Every young person has a unique set of skills and talents that, when cultivated, can create lasting change.

  • Talent & Skill Development
  • Collaboration & Community Engagement
  • Creative Expression
03
◆
Silab

Pagbabago ng Kabataan — The Youth's Blaze of Transformation

Fueled by passion and purpose, young people rise as advocates for change. Youth are not just the future — they are the present force for transformation in their communities.

  • Youth Advocacy Campaigns
  • Community Service & Volunteerism
  • Leadership & Empowerment Programs

SINAG is more than just an event — it is a legacy of light. Through Siklab, Alab, and Silab, we are building a movement that not only inspires but equips and empowers young people to rise above challenges and shine in their own ways.

Together, we will bring hope, make an impact, and ignite transformation.

A legacy of light. A movement of hope.
